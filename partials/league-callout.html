League formats offered

Multiple disciplines run through Tex Axes each season so throwers can grow into the format that fits them best.

Standard Hatchet League Classic sanctioned hatchet play for all skill levels.
Duals Hatchet League Partner-based hatchet competition with team coordination.
Big Axe League Heavier throws, bigger commitment, higher skill ceiling.
Knife Throwing League Official WKTL competition built around precision and control.
Knife Duals League Team-based knife competition for throwers who want more depth.
