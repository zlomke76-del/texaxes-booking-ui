Frequently asked questions

The core guest questions should be answered clearly before they book.

How much does axe throwing cost?

Axe throwing is $29 per hour per thrower. All activities and add-ons are subject to 8.25% sales tax.

Can we book for parties or work events?

Yes. We regularly host birthday parties, team events, and larger group outings. The experience scales easily for groups.

Do you serve food?

No — but many groups choose to cater in or bring food with them. We’re fully accommodating to outside food.

Is BYOB allowed?

Yes — we are BYOB. Beer, wine, and liquor are allowed. Guests must be 21+ to consume, and we ask that everyone drinks responsibly. A $5 per person BYOB fee applies.

Can I wear sandals or flip flops?

Closed-toe shoes are required for all throwers. This is for safety and insurance compliance — guests without proper footwear will not be allowed to throw.

Do you offer knife throwing?

Yes. WKTL knife throwing is available, with knife rental at $20 per set.

What does WATL certification mean?

WATL defines standardized rules, scoring, and competition formats. Tex Axes operates under those standards, offering a real throwing experience — not just a casual activity.

What does WKTL add to the experience?

WKTL introduces knife throwing as a second competitive lane, giving returning guests and more advanced throwers another reason to come back.

Is Tex Axes the only certified venue in Houston?

Yes. Tex Axes is the only WATL and WKTL certified venue in the Houston area.

Do guests need to sign a waiver?

Yes. All guests must complete a waiver before throwing. The process is quick and can be done ahead of arrival.
