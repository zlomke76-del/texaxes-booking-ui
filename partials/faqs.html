Frequently asked questions

The core guest questions should be answered clearly before they book.

How much does axe throwing cost?

Axe throwing is $29 per hour per thrower, plus 8.25% tax.

Is BYOB available?

Yes. BYOB is optional and costs $5.

Do you offer knife throwing?

Yes. WKTL knife rental is available for $20 per set.

What does WATL certification mean?

WATL publishes standardized rules, target design, scoring, distances, and competition formats, so Tex Axes is not just selling a casual activity — it is offering a venue aligned with recognized competitive standards.

What does WKTL add to the experience?

WKTL creates a second competitive lane through knife throwing, which gives repeat guests and more advanced throwers another reason to come back.

Is Tex Axes the only certified venue in Houston?

Yes. Tex Axes is the only certified WATL and WKTL venue in the Houston area, with the next comparable option roughly three hours away.

Do guests need to sign a waiver?

Yes. The waiver flow should stay visible and easy to complete before arrival.

Can we book for parties or work events?

Yes. The site structure supports casual bookings, birthday parties, work outings, and larger group event positioning.
