Built for Big Dave

Why this place exists

Tex Axes was built for my brother Dave. He loved throwing, competition, and bringing people together.

After we lost him in 2023, this became more than a venue. It became something we carry forward every week — in every league night, every throw, and every person who steps up to the line.
